About

Hi, I’m Albert Warepam—a passionate musician and content creator from Manipur , India. Music has been the rhythm of my life, and I love blending genres, especially through Hindi-English mashups that surprise and connect with people across cultures. With every beat and melody, I aim to create something new and soulful. Starting my journey on YouTube, I’ve grown a community of over 11k + subscribers who share my love for music. Whether it’s singing live on Omegle, creating viral mashups, or performing covers, I pour my heart into every note. On Instagram, I engage with 7,000+ followers through reels that document my musical evolution and daily life. Beyond music, I’m an 18-year-old balancing school life with my artistic dreams. I aspire to make a lasting impact, not just through covers, but with my own original compositions, which I’ll be sharing soon. Join me as I continue to explore, create, and share what I love most: music that speaks to the soul.
